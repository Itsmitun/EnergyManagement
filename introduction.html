Introduction to Intelligent Transport Systems

Understanding the fundamentals of ITS and their role in modern transportation

What are Intelligent Transport Systems?

Intelligent Transport Systems (ITS) represent a revolutionary approach to transportation management that leverages Internet of Things (IoT) sensors, advanced data networks, and real-time communication technologies to create safer, more efficient, and environmentally sustainable transportation networks.

These systems integrate various technologies including vehicle-to-vehicle (V2V) and vehicle-to-infrastructure (V2I) communications, smart traffic management, and energy optimization techniques to address the growing challenges of urban mobility.
