IoV, CAV, V2X & V2G Technologies

Next-generation technologies shaping the future of transportation

Future of Transportation

The future of transportation lies in the integration of advanced technologies that create interconnected, intelligent, and energy-efficient transportation ecosystems. These technologies represent the next evolution of ITS capabilities, enabling unprecedented levels of safety, efficiency, and sustainability.

The convergence of Internet of Things, artificial intelligence, and advanced communication technologies is creating a new paradigm for transportation that goes beyond traditional vehicle-centric approaches.

Internet of Vehicles (IoV)

Network of connected vehicles that share real-time data for improved traffic management and safety. Enables predictive maintenance and optimized routing through comprehensive vehicle-to-everything connectivity.

Key Features:

  • Real-time vehicle data sharing
  • Predictive maintenance systems
  • Dynamic route optimization
  • Fleet management integration

Connected and Autonomous Vehicles (CAV)

Self-driving vehicles with advanced communication capabilities that can coordinate with each other and infrastructure for optimal traffic flow. Combines autonomous driving with intelligent connectivity.

Key Features:

  • Level 4-5 autonomous driving
  • Multi-vehicle coordination
  • Infrastructure integration
  • Safety-critical communication

Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X)

Comprehensive communication framework enabling vehicles to communicate with pedestrians, infrastructure, networks, and other vehicles. Creates a unified communication ecosystem for transportation.

Key Features:

  • Multi-modal communication
  • Pedestrian safety integration
  • Infrastructure coordination
  • Network connectivity

Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G)

Bidirectional energy flow between electric vehicles and the power grid, providing frequency regulation and energy storage services. Transforms vehicles into mobile energy storage units.

Key Features:

  • Bidirectional energy flow
  • Grid stabilization services
  • Renewable energy integration
  • Economic incentives for owners
